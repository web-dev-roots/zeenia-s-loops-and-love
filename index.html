Our story

Made by hands,
felt by heart

Hi, I'm Zeenia — the maker behind every loop and knot you see here. What started as a quiet hobby during long evenings turned into a full love affair with crochet.

Every piece I create is made by hand, with carefully chosen yarns and lots of patience. No two pieces are exactly alike — and that's exactly the point.
